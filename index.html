FAQ

What is SwisSafe and where will you be regulated?

SwisSafe is a Swiss-regulated financial institution.

Your assets are stored with our regulated partner banks and third-party custodians.

Yes, though we're unable at present to accept companies domiciled in the United States and OFAC-sanctioned countries.

What fees should I be aware of?

Our fees will be simple, reasonable and transparent. There will be modest platform and transaction fees.

How will you provide better service than other banks?

We aim to provide a boutique and bespoke level of service. You will be able to reach staff including our founders directly. We will understand your business rather than subject you to generic KYC and KYT procedures.
